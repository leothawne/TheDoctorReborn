♟ The Doctor Reborn ♟

A Minecraft RPG plugin based on Doctor Who.

❯ Reborn as a Time Lord victorious!

The Doctor Reborn allows you to regenerate instead of dying.

Become a powerful Time Lord with 12 lives left for you to enjoy and have fun with your friends.

❯ Looking for downloads?

Download the latest version Here or Here.

This plugin works on Bukkit & Spigot!

❯ Need support?

The plugin is on its initial versions. It will be very common to find bugs.
Always check the server console for detailed info about internal errors.
If you would like to report a new issue, visit the issues page to report and/or look for reported issues.
