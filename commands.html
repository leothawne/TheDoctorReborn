♟ The Doctor Reborn ♟

Regenerate 12 times as a Time Lord.

Commands for everyone:

  • /reborn - Show all commands for The Doctor Reborn.
  • /reborn version - Show plugin version.
  • /reborn info - Show your regeneration status.
  • /reborn force - Force your regeneration process.
  • /reborn lock [on/off] - Lock/unlock your regeneration ability.
  • /reborn contact - In case you want to report a bug or something. (< v0.3.0)
  • /reborn home - Define your home location. (< v0.3.0)
  • /reborn recipes - Show a list of craftable items. (< v0.3.0)
  • /reborn ch <amount> - Clear your main hand.
  • /reborn tp - A teleportation tool for multiple worlds server. (< v0.3.0)
  • You can also use /reborn as /rb.

Subcommands for '/reborn home' (< v0.3.0):

  • /reborn home - Teleport you to your home location (when it's set) (< v0.3.0).
  • /reborn home set - Set your home location (< v0.3.0).
  • /reborn home del - Delete your home location (< v0.3.0).
  • /reborn home where - Tell where your home location is (< v0.3.0).
  • This features work with multiple worlds/multiverses (< v0.3.0).

Commands for administrators:

  • /rebornadmin - Administration commands for The Doctor Reborn.
  • /rebornadmin version - Check for new updates.
  • /rebornadmin info <timelord> - Show the regeneration status of a Time Lord.
  • /rebornadmin force <timelord> - Force a Time Lord to regenerate. (Please, use this with caution!)
  • /rebornadmin toggle <timelord> [on/off] - Toggle regeneration cycle of a Time Lord.
  • /rebornadmin give <item> <amount> - An easy way of getting TDR items (< v0.3.0).
  • /rebornadmin reload - Reload the configuration file (< v0.3.0).
  • /rebornadmin update <timelord> - Kick the specified Time Lord to update its account (< v0.3.0).
  • You can also use /rebornadmin as /rba.
