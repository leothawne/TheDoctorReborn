♟ The Doctor Reborn ♟

A Minecraft RPG plugin based on Doctor Who.

Commands for everyone:

  • /reborn - Show all commands for The Doctor Reborn.
  • /reborn version - Show plugin version.
  • /reborn info - Show your regeneration status.
  • /reborn force - Force your regeneration process.
  • /reborn lock [on/off] - Lock/unlock your regeneration ability.
  • /reborn contact - In case you want to report a bug or something.
  • /reborn home - Define your home location (why not build a time lord's temple and use this to set your home location?).
  • /reborn recipes - Show a list of craftable items.
  • /reborn ch <amount> - Clear your main hand.
  • /reborn tp - A teleportation tool for multiple worlds server.
  • You can also use /reborn as /rb.

Subcommands for '/reborn home':

  • /reborn home - Teleport you to your home location (when it's set).
  • /reborn home set - Set your home location.
  • /reborn home del - Delete your home location.
  • /reborn home where - Tell where your home location is.
  • This features work with multiple worlds/multiverses.

Commands for administrators:

  • /rebornadmin - Administration commands for The Doctor Reborn.
  • /rebornadmin version - Check for new updates.
  • /rebornadmin info <timelord> - Show the regeneration status of a Time Lord.
  • /rebornadmin force <timelord> - Force a Time Lord to regenerate. (Please, use this with caution!)
  • /rebornadmin toggle <timelord> [on/off] - Toggle regeneration cycle of a Time Lord.
  • /rebornadmin give <item> <amount> - An easy way of getting TDR items.
  • /rebornadmin reload - Reload the configuration file.
  • /rebornadmin update <timelord> - Kick the specified Time Lord to update its account.
  • You can also use /rebornadmin as /rba.
